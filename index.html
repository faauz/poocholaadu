About Poocholamadu

Poocholamadu is a picturesque village nestled in the heart of nature, known for its rich cultural heritage and warm community spirit. With a history dating back several centuries, our village has preserved its traditional charm while embracing modern amenities.

The name "Poocholamadu" came by the pond situated in our village , reflecting the natural beauty that surrounds our community. Our village is home to diverse flora and fauna, pristine water bodies, and hills that create a mesmerizing landscape.

Notable People

Cultural Guardian

Rajasekaran Pillai

The oldest living resident who has preserved ancient folk songs and stories for future generations.

Master Artisan

Lakshmi Devi

Renowned textile artist whose handloom creations have been exhibited internationally and preserved traditional weaving techniques.

Agricultural Innovator

Muthu Kumar

Pioneer in sustainable farming practices who introduced organic methods that increased crop yield while preserving soil health.

Educational Leader

Dr. Anita Rajan

Founded the village's first school and scholarship program, helping hundreds of local children access quality education.

Health Pioneer

Dr. Poornima Sharma

Established the village's first medical clinic, blending traditional herbal knowledge with modern medical practices.

Community Leader

Thiru Chandran

Former village head who led community development projects including water conservation and renewable energy initiatives.
