About Poocholamadu

History & Heritage

Poocholamadu is a picturesque village nestled in the heart of Malappuram district, Kerala. The name "Poocholamadu" derives from Malayalam words meaning "flower pond," reflecting the village's natural beauty and abundance of flora.

Cultural Background

Our village is home to a diverse community that celebrates unity in diversity. With a rich cultural heritage spanning centuries, Poocholamadu has been a center of learning, trade, and spiritual harmony.

  • By Train: Malappuram Railway Station (15 km)
